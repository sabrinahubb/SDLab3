About me

Hi, my name is Diego Rivera, and I am currently in my senior year at the University of Iowa studying Electrical Engineering with a focus in Power Systems.

Hobbies

If I’m not on my bike I’m usually outrunning, listening to music, welding, or working on a circuit. All of these things have played an integral role in shaping my identity.

Biking

When it comes to bikes I’m obsessed. I spend quite a bit of my day to-day time on my bike exploring. I’ve biked in the mountains of Colorado to the bike parks of Arkansas and the trails in Wisconsin. Not only do I enjoy riding bikes, but I also really enjoy making them from scratch. I’ve spent the last two semesters, and a summer, learning to miter, weld, and design steel bicycle frames. This has given me a level of understanding that I never expected to know, but have thoroughly enjoyed learning.
