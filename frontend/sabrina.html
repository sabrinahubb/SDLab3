Audio

The original reason I began studying electrical engineering is because I wanted to design synthesizers, speakers, DACs, amplifiers, and preamplifiers. Basically the whole audio chain. A lot of my fascination with the electrical world is rooted in my connection to sound, signals, and how they manifest themselves in the real world. Music is very real, and personal, connection to electricity and I desired a deeper understanding of the magic that goes into audio signal reproduction. This was my gateway into the world of electricity, electromagnetics, microelectronics, and power systems.
